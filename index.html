Overview

Kenya has a rich higher education system with both public and private universities offering diverse programs. Below are some examples:

University of Nairobi

The oldest and largest university in Kenya, offering a wide range of courses in arts, sciences, engineering, and medicine.

Kenyatta University

Known for its strong focus on education, research, and technology innovation.

Strathmore University

A leading private university, highly regarded for business, IT, and law programs.
